The Solution: Automated AI-Powered Tracking

Computer Vision Meets Surgical Workflow

SurgiGuard AI uses advanced computer vision and deep learning to automatically track every surgical instrument throughout the procedure in real-time.

  • Real-Time Counting: AI algorithms maintain accurate counts without manual intervention
  • Instant Alerts: Immediate notification if count discrepancies are detected before closing
  • Complete Audit Trail: Full video documentation and tracking logs for every procedure
